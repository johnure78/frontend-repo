Who We Are

God's Invasion Outreach embodies our role as God's response to humanity, extending compassion and support to those in urgent need.

Our Identity

God's Invasion Outreach is a Christian charity organization dedicated to reaching out with love, faith, and practical support. Since our founding, we have worked to bring hope and healing to the most vulnerable in our society, including orphans, inmates, and impoverished communities.

Our Vision

We emphasize God's interest in every individual's destiny, particularly those in orphanages, schools, hospitals, prisons, and streets. We aim to extend compassion, love, and support to those in urgent needs.

Our Mission

  • Reaching orphanages, schools, hospitals, prisons and streets.
  • Inspiring a movement of compassion among believers.
  • Fostering positive change through practical support.

Our Programs

Guided by compassion and Christ's love, our outreach programs serve widows, prisoners, orphaned children, and struggling communities, bringing hope, care, and renewed purpose to those in need.

Prison Outreach

Through our prison outreach program, we bring hope, restoration, and spiritual guidance to inmates, reminding them that redemption and transformation are always possible through Christ.
